Download

LOAF provides images of three different resolutions and their corresponding annotations： resolution512（512*512），resolution1k（1024*1024），resolution2k（2048*2048），resolution3k（2952*2952）. In each resolution, LOAF split into five sets: train (7/28 indoor/outdoor scenes, 29,569 images), val seen (1/2 indoor/outdoor scenes, 1,700 images), val unseen (2/3 indoor/outdoor scenes, 2,900 images), test seen (2/3 indoor/outdoor scenes, 2,774 images), test unseen (2/9 indoor/outdoor scenes, 6,599 images). We provide for all annotations in a format of COCO.
